Winchester Proposition 2½ override voting history

Interactive visualization of every Proposition 2½ override and debt exclusion vote in Winchester, Massachusetts, from 1988 through 2023.

About the data

The dataset covers 33 ballot questions across 25 elections over 35 years. It includes precinct-level results for all eight Winchester precincts, with vote totals, turnout percentages and outcomes for each question.

Results are broken down by decade, override type (debt exclusion, general override and Proposition 2½ override) and individual ballot question. The timeline view shows every vote at a glance, with tooltips displaying the question description, approval percentage and outcome.

Key findings

- Winchester voters rejected 16 of 19 ballot questions from 1988 through 2004. Since 2007, 12 of 14 questions have passed.
- Debt exclusions passed 8 of 14 times (57 percent). General overrides passed just 2 of 14 times (14 percent). Recent Proposition 2½ overrides have passed all 5 times since 2019.
- The 2007 Hamilton Farm acquisition produced the widest margin of victory (+3,774 votes) among all 33 questions. The 2022 Northeast Vocational High School question earned the highest approval rate (91.9 percent).
- School-related ballot questions have passed 7 of 8 times since 1998.

Source

Town of Winchester, History of Proposition 2½ Override Referenda Questions, 1988–2023.
